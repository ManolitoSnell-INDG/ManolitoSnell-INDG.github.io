MovieAdapter
A scaleform movie which acts like a simple-simple facade. Here you may register a dedicated movie mediator which will act upon a component as is available in the SWF (that is the MVC part). We work without notifications, instead we have some sort of event system and there are no commands available, you may call directly the methods for a given component.
