Triangulate(IEnumerableHxVector3D, IEnumerableHxVector3D, ListHxVector3D, ListInt32)
This class implements triangulation according to Computational Geometry 2nd rev, chapter 3.2 and 3.3 Assumptions about the polygons: - Greater y values are higher. - Vertices are ordered counter-clockwise. - Do not close the loop (ie there is no need to have the last vertex be equal to the first). - Holes have their vertices ordered clockwise. This method is not thread-safe!
