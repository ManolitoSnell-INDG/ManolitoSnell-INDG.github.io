SoundClipMediator Class
Although Flash/Scaleform is perfectly able to play audio clips, Scaleform under iOS isn't able to do so (atm. 18 aug. 2014). For that we have to fall back to Unity playing audio clips. That is where this mediator comes in, it will respond to
Copy
PLAY_AUDIO
notifications and respond to it accordingly.
