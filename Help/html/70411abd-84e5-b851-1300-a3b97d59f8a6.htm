CompoundExpression
Evaluator for compound expression. A compound expression is any string that has sections of actual expression in them bounded by curly braces, e.g. "1, 2, {3+2}, {4/2}". There is no restriction on the string outside the curly braces. Compound expressions always evaluate to strings, with the embedded expressions replaced by their results converted to strings.
